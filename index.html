About Me

Hey! I'm a second year undergraduate student at the University of California, Irvine studying Computer Science and Engineering.

My primary research interest is in examining and interpreting generation of transformer-based LMs at decode time to improve output quality and accuracy. I'm also broadly interested in optimizing model inference and training at the edge compute level through hardware/software co-design.
